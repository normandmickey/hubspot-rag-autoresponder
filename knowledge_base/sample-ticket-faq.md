# Sample Ticket FAQ

## Access issues
If a customer cannot access their account, acknowledge the issue, ask for the exact error message, and confirm the email address on the account.

## Billing issues
If the message mentions billing, refunds, duplicate charges, or invoices, do not make promises. Acknowledge the issue and say the billing team will review the details.

## Setup questions
If the customer asks about setup, explain the onboarding steps at a high level and ask for the specific use case if they need tailored guidance.
